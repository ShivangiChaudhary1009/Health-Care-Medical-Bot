EYE INFECTIONS TREATMENT


Quality Healthcare At Your Fingertips


Eyes are one of the most sensitive parts of our human body. Consequently, they always demand additional care. Especially from dust, radiation, sunlight, UV rays, and the most harmful of them all – infections. Eye infections usually happen when microorganisms like fungi, bacteria, and viruses penetrate any part of your eye. This includes the clear front surface of the eye (cornea), the moist membrane (outer eye), and inner eyelids(conjunctiva).



Treatment

  • Many common viral eye infections get cured on their own. Other eye infections are curable with prompt treatment such as antibiotics or antiviral eye drops, ointments, and compresses. Other severe viral eye infections require steroid eye drops to deal with related inflammation.
  • How Depending on the cause and the condition of your eye-infection, the doctor may also prescribe some antibiotics or antiviral medications by oral administration.
  • Nevertheless, one must consult a doctor immediately when contaminated with eye infection to avoid any undesired harm to the eyes.
