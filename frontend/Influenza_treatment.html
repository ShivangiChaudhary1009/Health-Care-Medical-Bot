Wondering what will work for you? Or What is the best treatment for influenza? Or whether or not can you manage flu on your own? All these questions need clear answers so that you can alleviate symptoms like fever, aches, cough, and flu symptoms.



Treatment

If you are looking for instant relief, you can choose decongestant sprays. But, stop using one after around three days to avoid reviving the condition all over again. Some doctors suggest a saline spray that helps to clear out thick mucus in the nasal passages. You can use it for an extended period without falling into any side effects.


  • An occasional cough helps to clear out occasional phlegm. But, if your cough is persistent, then it is important that you start taking some cough medicine in combination with decongestants, antihistamines, analgesics, cough suppressants.
